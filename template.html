Superhuman Genes

This is Superhuman genes report. In this report you can find the information about "superhuman" genes that you possess.

Based on the spreadsheet by George Church.

It's important to understand that having the "superability" gene variant does not guarantee that you will definitely have the corresponding superpower. This gene variant may only predispose you to the trait, meaning that it increases the likelihood of developing the superpower, but it is not a guarantee.

This is because genes can have varying degrees of expressivity (which refers to how strongly a gene is expressed in an individual) and penetrance (which refers to the proportion of individuals with a particular gene variant who actually express the associated trait). Even if you possess the "superability" gene, other factors like gene interactions and environmental factors can also affect whether the trait is manifested. So, while having the gene variant may increase your chances of developing the superpower, it's not a certainty.
